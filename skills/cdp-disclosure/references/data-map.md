# CDP theme → Soapbox data source map

Themes are stable across CDP questionnaire revisions even when question numbers move.
Bind each theme to the current year's question refs at engagement start.

| Theme | What CDP asks | Soapbox source | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Introduction & boundary | reporting entity, boundary, sector | org + fund structure (user) | must match GHG inventory boundary |
| Governance | board oversight, management roles, incentives | user / org docs on file | evidence: charters, ESG policy |
| Risk & opportunity identification | process, time horizons, materiality | tcfd-reporting skill output | CDP is TCFD/ISSB-aligned; reuse scenario work |
| Physical risk exposure | asset/portfolio hazard exposure, financial impact | Moody's RMS / physrisk MCPs | quantify where possible |
| Transition risk & opportunity | policy (BPS penalties), market, technology | bps-compliance (penalty $), CRREM (stranding) | penalty exposure is a strong quantified answer |
| Business strategy & transition plan | climate in strategy, transition plan, scenario analysis | Audette roadmaps, portfolio-analysis | committed decarb CapEx is Management-level evidence |
| Targets | absolute/intensity targets, base year, progress | CRREM pathway targets, org commitments | note SBTi validation status if any |
| Scope 1 & 2 emissions | inventory, methodology, verification status | scope3-accounting skill | must reconcile exactly |
| Scope 3 emissions | categories incl. 13 (leased assets), 15 (investments) | scope3-accounting skill (PCAF) | real estate: Cat 13/15 are the material ones |
| Energy | consumption by fuel, renewable share | ESPM / Arcadia / utility data | portfolio totals + intensity |
| Water (RE sector) | withdrawal/consumption where material | utility data where metered | often a gap — flag honestly |
| Emissions reduction initiatives | implemented measures, savings | Audette measures, retrofit history | quantify annual CO₂e savings |
| Carbon pricing / offsets | internal price, credits | user | usually N/A for RE mid-market |
| Verification | third-party assurance of inventory | external assurer (user) | required for top scoring bands |
| Public disclosure & engagement | reports, tenant/value-chain engagement | sustainability reports on file, green leases | green lease clauses = engagement evidence |
